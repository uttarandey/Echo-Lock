EchoLock Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Overview

EchoLock is an offline-first Android memory puzzle game published by the EchoLock developer. The game is designed to keep normal gameplay progress on your device while using selected Google services for ads, analytics, crash diagnostics, notifications, consent, and app platform features.

Data EchoLock Stores Locally

EchoLock may store the following on your device:

  • Local player profile name and local player ID.
  • Progress, unlocked levels, stars, scores, coins, inventory, shop ownership, daily reward state, settings, and tutorial state.
  • Local analytics event names and gameplay metadata used to improve the app during testing and support.
  • Local crash or diagnostic logs used to investigate app stability.

Firebase, Analytics, And Crash Reporting

EchoLock uses Firebase services, including Firebase Cloud Messaging, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may process app-instance identifiers, installation identifiers, device or diagnostic metadata, gameplay event metadata, messaging tokens, crash reports, non-fatal error reports, and related app stability information.

Analytics is used to understand broad app behavior such as app opens, screen opens, level starts, level completions, reward requests, and ad events. Crashlytics is used to diagnose crashes and non-fatal errors so the game can be repaired.

Ads And Consent

EchoLock uses Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK for optional rewarded ads and capped interstitial ads. AdMob and related Google services may process advertising identifiers, device identifiers, approximate ad delivery information, consent choices, ad requests, impressions, clicks, and reward completion signals.

EchoLock also uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request or update consent when required by your region and by advertising policy.

Cloud Save

EchoLock is currently offline-first. Cloud save and account login are not active in the current MVP release. If cloud save is added later, this policy and the in-app disclosure will be updated before that feature is released.

Data Sharing

EchoLock does not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by third-party services used by the app, including Firebase, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob, Google Mobile Ads SDK, Google User Messaging Platform, and Google Play services.

Data EchoLock Does Not Request

EchoLock does not request contacts, SMS, phone call permission, precise location, camera, microphone, or sensitive personal data for gameplay.

Children

EchoLock is not configured as a child-directed app unless and until the Google Play target audience and content rating process declares otherwise.

Data Deletion

You can delete local gameplay data by using Settings > Reset Progress in the app, or by clearing the app data from Android system settings. For questions or deletion requests related to future cloud features, contact support.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when EchoLock changes its data practices, SDK usage, ads behavior, or release status. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
